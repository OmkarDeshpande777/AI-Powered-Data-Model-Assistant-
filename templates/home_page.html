How to Use DataVerse?

  1. Data Cleaning: Click on the "Data Cleaning" button and upload your dataset (.csv file). Choose the columns to remove and download the cleaned file.
  2. Visualization & Insights: Upload your dataset and generate graphical representations of the data for easy analysis.
  3. Model Recommendation & Tuning: Receive AI model recommendations based on your dataset and fine-tune parameters for better performance.
  4. Navigation: Use the back button on each page to return to the home screen.
  5. Download Results: After each step, you can download your processed dataset or insights.
